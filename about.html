What Affects Wine Quality?

Wine quality is influenced by a combination of chemistry and sensory balance, including acidity profile, alcohol level, sulphates, sugar, and pH.

Why It Matters in Industry

Data-assisted quality prediction supports production consistency, pricing strategy, and faster quality assurance for wineries and distributors.

Why Use Machine Learning

ML helps identify non-obvious feature interactions and estimate likely quality bands from laboratory measurements before expert sensory evaluation.
