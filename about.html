About the Platform

This platform bridges chemical analysis and sensory evaluation of wine quality using machine learning and transparent, research-backed logic.

🍇 What is Wine Quality?

Wine quality is traditionally assessed by human experts (scores 3–8). This subjective process creates inconsistency that ML can address.

🤖 Why Prediction Matters

ML provides consistent, data-driven evaluation by analyzing chemical properties — alcohol, acidity, sulphates — that correlate with expert scores.

🏭 Industry Applications

Automated quality assessment helps wineries optimize production, maintain standards, and reduce expert panel costs.

🎯 Our Approach

We combine published research with interactive simulation. The Explorer uses rule-based logic derived from 6,497 wine samples, mimicking a trained XGBoost classifier.
