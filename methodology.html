Data Preprocessing

Data cleaning handles missing values and outliers, while scaling aligns feature ranges for robust model training and fair feature contribution.

Feature Selection

Core features such as alcohol, volatile acidity, sulphates, and pH are prioritized due to strong explanatory power in wine quality datasets.

Models (Conceptual)

  • Logistic Regression for interpretable baseline classification.
  • XGBoost for high-performance non-linear learning.

Evaluation Metrics

Performance is assessed with accuracy, precision, recall, F1-score, and confusion matrix analysis.
