Dataset Overview

Typical wine quality datasets contain physicochemical measurements and a quality score. These measurements are used as model features to estimate likely quality outcomes.

Feature Engineering & Model Concepts

Features such as alcohol, volatile acidity, sulphates, and pH are normalized and analyzed. In production systems, models like logistic regression, random forests, or gradient boosting may be applied to capture complex relationships.

Evaluation Metrics

Model performance is commonly evaluated using accuracy, precision, recall, F1-score, and confusion matrices. For regression-style quality estimation, MAE and RMSE can also be used.
