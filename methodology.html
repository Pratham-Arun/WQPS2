📋 Feature Selection

Correlation analysis reveals alcohol (r = +0.44), density (r = −0.31), and volatile acidity (r = −0.27) are the strongest predictors.

🧪 Models Used

Logistic Regression provides a linear baseline. XGBoost captures non-linear interactions, achieving higher accuracy on multi-class quality prediction.

📐 Evaluation Metrics

Accuracy: Overall correctness.
Precision: Of predicted "high quality", how many truly are.
Recall: Of all "high quality" wines, how many were identified.

⚙️ Simulation Engine

The Explorer uses a rule-based scoring engine derived from the trained model's feature importances to provide transparent, explainable predictions.
