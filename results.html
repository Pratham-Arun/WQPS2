Key Findings & Results

Analysis of 6,497 wine samples reveals strong patterns between chemical composition and quality.

Feature Importance

Correlation with Quality

🏆 Top Predictor

Alcohol has the highest positive correlation (r = +0.44) with quality. Higher alcohol wines consistently score better.

⚠️ Quality Killer

Volatile Acidity is a strong negative factor (r = −0.27). High levels produce vinegar-like off-flavors.

📈 Model Performance

XGBoost achieves ~72% accuracy on exact scores and 90%+ when classifying High (≥7) vs Standard quality.

Quality Score Distribution

Most wines cluster around scores 5–6. Premium wines (7–9) make up ~20% of the dataset.
